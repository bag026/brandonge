Brandon Ge

Hello there! This is Brandon, I’m a game developer around 6 years with much experience and passion for bringing virtual worlds to life. Game development for me is not just about coding; it's an art of crafting experiences that transport players to other realms, a blend of technology and creativity that fires up my enthusiasm every day. Imagine a blend between intricate lines of code and the vibrant chaos of a canvas - that's where I find my stride. It's a path that's as challenging as it is rewarding. Just like a well-balanced game, my life has its own dynamics. There's the intensity of the development phase, comparable to the final quarter of a basketball game where every second counts. Speaking of basketball, it's my go-to activity after a day of game development. Hitting the court helps me clear my mind and keeps me energized. It's also where I draw parallels between my sport and work ethic; both require teamwork, strategy, and a fair share of agility and skill.
